## What the per-m² price includes

Two coats of premium washable paint on walls and ceilings, repair of minor defects, full protection of floors and furniture, labour and final cleanup. Not included (quoted as separate line items in the same visit): wall smoothing or gotelé removal, door lacquering, radiator enamelling.

## What pushes the price up

Five concrete factors: wall condition (cracks and damp need extra prep: +10-15%), high ceilings in classic buildings (+10-15%), intense colours or dark-to-light changes needing a third coat (+10%), a furnished flat versus empty (+10%), and the paint quality you choose. A serious quote itemises each one — be wary of round numbers quoted sight-unseen.

## Comparing painter quotes in Madrid

Ask every painter for three things in writing: the breakdown (area, rooms, prep work, paint brand and model, number of coats), the schedule with dates, and whether the price is fixed or estimated.
