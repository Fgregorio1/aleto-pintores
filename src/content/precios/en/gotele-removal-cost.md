## Why the range is wide (€9 to €15 per m²)

Because "gotelé" is really two different materials. **Temple-based** texture (1960s-80s buildings) soaks off with water — fewer hours, bottom of the range. **Plastic-paint** texture (80s-90s) doesn't dissolve: it's machine-sanded or skimmed over with 2-3 plaster coats — more hours and material, top of the range. Wall condition adds the rest: cracks, damp or loose plaster need repair before smoothing.

## Is it worth the money?

In Madrid, measurably yes. Smooth walls are among the first things buyers and tenants register; property portals consistently list them among the highest-return pre-sale improvements alongside kitchen and bathroom. For €1,800–2,600 an 80 m² flat moves visually from "needs renovating" to "move-in ready" — a difference that repays itself in the sale price or monthly rent.
