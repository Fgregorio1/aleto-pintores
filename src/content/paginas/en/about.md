## Why Aleto Pintores exists

Madrid's painting market runs on opaque quotes: prices nobody publishes, figures that grow mid-job, quality impossible to compare. Aleto was founded in 2026 to do the exact opposite — **publish the prices**, fix the quote before starting, and finish every job well enough that the next one comes from a recommendation.

## How we work

Every job follows the same method: free visit with real measurements, fixed written quote within 24 hours detailing paint brands and coats, complete protection of the home, execution on the agreed schedule, and a final wall-by-wall review with you. We charge on completion, once the work is reviewed and accepted.
