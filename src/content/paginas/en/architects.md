## The problem we solve for studios

An interiors project lives or dies in the finishes: the exact colour, the prescribed sheen, a wall that the LED strip doesn't betray. Too often the weak link is execution — the contractor who "interprets" the colour, flattens the sheen, or delivers late and leaves the studio explaining itself to its client. Our job is making sure that never happens: we execute what's specified, as specified, when committed. And when we spot a technical problem in the spec — a substrate needing a different primer, a sheen that will flash — we raise it with the studio before executing, not after.

## Samples and sign-off before execution

No aesthetic decision gets made from a catalogue. We apply real samples of the specified colour and sheen on the project's actual substrate — not on card — and validate them with the studio under the space's real light, natural and artificial. On sensitive projects we prepare larger sample panels per room. It's an hour of work that prevents the most expensive conversation in any project: the repaint after handover.

## Site coordination and deadlines that hold

We join the general programme and work with the other trades, not against them: sequencing agreed with carpentry and installations, protection of work already executed, and real flexibility when the site needs it. Committed deadlines are signed — we know our delay is everyone's delay. The studio gets one site contact, photo progress reports, and zero surprises at the site inspection.

## Documentation without chasing

Datasheets for every applied system, liability insurance, H&S paperwork where the project direction requires it, invoice and written warranty. The admin side arrives complete and unprompted — a contractor without papers isn't a contractor a serious studio can approve.
