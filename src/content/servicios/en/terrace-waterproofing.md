## Why liquid membrane is our default recommendation

It forms a continuous seamless layer — no joints or overlaps, which is where sheet systems fail — it's walkable, takes colour, and applies without demolition over the existing surface. At critical points (drains, corners, wall junctions) we reinforce with mesh: that detail is the difference between a system lasting 3 years and 15.

## Madrid weather is hard on terraces

Freeze-thaw cycles in winter and extreme roof temperatures in summer punish old membranes. If a stain has already appeared on the ceiling below, the water has been working for a while — the earlier it's treated, the cheaper the fix. If your community is scaffolding the building for [facade painting](/en/services/facade-community-painting/), that's the perfect moment to treat the roof too.
