## Why removal is trickier than it looks

The paper hides what's underneath. A modern vinyl paper over sound walls comes off clean in hours; a 1980s paper with two layers and hardened glue can pull the plaster off if stripped dry. Our job is getting it off without damaging the substrate — proper steam, proper soak times — and leaving a wall that's truly ready for its next finish.

## What finish to choose afterwards

Three usual routes: **paint directly** if the wall comes out sound (cheapest), **skim and paint** for a perfect finish or damaged walls (see [gotelé removal & smoothing](/en/services/gotele-removal-wall-smoothing/) — same technique), or **re-paper** a feature wall with a modern print. We'll recommend honestly at the free visit based on what the wall actually needs.
