## What a community should demand before painting the facade

Three things: an honest diagnosis of the substrate (repainting is not the same as structural crack repair), an itemised quote the board can actually compare, and a realistic schedule that respects residents. Our quotes always separate access equipment, repairs and coating system — so the community knows exactly what it's paying for.

## The right coating for each facade

Sound render gets a high-durability smooth acrylic; micro-cracked surfaces get an elastic crack-bridging coating; brick and salty substrates get breathable pliolite paint. A facade is repainted every 10-15 years — the technical choice matters far more than saving €1/m² on material.
