## Why remove gotelé before selling or renting?

Smooth walls are the single most visible upgrade in a Spanish flat. To buyers and tenants, gotelé reads as "dated, needs renovating"; smooth painted walls read as "move-in ready" — and that perception shows up directly in offers and rent. For €1,800–2,600, an 80 m² flat changes category. If you're an international owner preparing a flat for the Madrid market, this plus fresh paint is the highest-return combination available.

## How we keep the dust under control

The horror stories about gotelé removal are about dust. Our process is built around avoiding it: rooms sealed with plastic sheeting, sanders connected to industrial vacuum extraction, daily cleanup, and furniture wrapped completely. The flat stays livable through the whole job.

## What determines the final price?

Two things. **The gotelé type**: water-soluble temple (common in older buildings) strips off cheaply; plastic-paint gotelé needs sanding or skimming over — more hours, higher price. **The wall condition underneath**: cracks, damp marks or crumbling plaster need repair before smoothing.
